About Yamaha

Yamaha is a famous company that makes motorcycles.

Yamaha Corporation is a major Japanese company founded in 1887 by Torakusu Yamaha, known worldwide for producing musical instruments, audio equipment, and professional sound technology, while its related company, Yamaha Motor Company, focuses on motorcycles, boats, and other motorized products; together, the Yamaha name represents a blend of innovation, quality, and performance across both music and engineering industries.

Yamaha Corporation is a globally recognized Japanese brand founded in 1887 by Torakusu Yamaha, originally as a piano and reed organ manufacturer, and it has since grown into one of the world’s leading producers of musical instruments, audio systems, and professional sound equipment; alongside it, Yamaha Motor Company, which split off in 1955, has become a major force in the automotive world with its motorcycles, marine engines, and recreational vehicles, and together these companies have built a strong reputation for precision engineering, innovation, and reliability, making Yamaha a trusted name in both the music industry and high-performance machinery worldwide.
